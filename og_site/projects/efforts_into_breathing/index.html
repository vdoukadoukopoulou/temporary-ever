efforts into breathing compiles three works: breath one, exercises on breath and aeration all exploring breathing as a medium

breath one was created as a part of MADCOW, and it takes the form of a soft plastic performative installation, it was created to explore the differences between the industrial architectural space and the tactile materiality of it.

exercises on breath is a live performative installation of Samuel Beckett's breath for 6 audience members, 3 performers ,scent and lights to be repeated 3 times in 3 spaces.the work builds upon the Beckett piece and explores the idea of abstracting life through the senses, Beckett's piece in 8 lines successfully captures a whole life from birth to death and reduces it into it's fundamental functions : breath, sound and light. exercises on breath goes a step further by eliminating the stage set up, the placement of the rubbish and substituting the vagitus cry with the smell of vanilla.

aeration was created as a part of Sonic Labs: Sonic Air Spaces and continues the exploration of the differences between the industrial architectural space and the tactile materiality of it. in the form of a large plastic inflatable installation, aeration inflates by use of organ pump to transforms the space, slowly taking over and separating the crowd, forcing it to explore the upstairs.
